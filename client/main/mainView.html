What is this?

RTS Chess is a simple single page web app to play chess. But not any chess, RTS chess; or "Real Time Stratagy" chess. Although most of the rulse of chess still apply the major diffrence is your not limited by how many moves you can make in a sinle turn. With RTS chess you can move as many peices as you want, however every time you move a peices you have to wait a specific amount of time before you can move that same piece again so be carefull.

With this system each peices has one of three classes which will determine their wait time.

    Class 3 : Pawns and King : 5 second
    Class 2 : Bishop, Knights, Rooks : 5.5 second
    Class 1 : Queen/s : 6 second

Not a big deal?

Well, its not, as long as you remeber to be aware of the time. If you use your queen to move into possition, your opponent can move his own pawn into strike possition immidiatly after. Giving him a one second window to take your queen before you can do anything so be prepaired
